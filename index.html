About Us

Highlyfe Collective is a label, hospitality, and media production company. The Collective brings together the best in live music, creative design, and immersive production. Our curated gatherings are designed for a select group of tastemakers, music enthusiasts, and influencers, offering a unique blend of artistic expression. Specializing in exclusivised services allows us to focus on creating higher quality experiences.

Our Services

Private Events

We host and manage private events at exclusive venues across Los Angeles. Album release parties, music videos, security, and modeling shoots are all done in-house.

Clubbing & Promotion

Highlyfe promoters work at the most exclusive clubs in West Hollywood through companies like Hwood Group, Tao, and Ten Five Hospitality. Our promoters ensure our clients have fun while staying safe and avoid scams or overcharging.

Venue Rentals

The Collective provides high-end sets and venues for its members. Whether it's luxury cars, houses, yachts, and more; we have the access you need to make quality content in exclusive places.

Artist Management & Marketing

Highlyfe Collective is an independent label and provides management along with music development services to upcoming artists. We utilize major label and industry connections to help distinguish independent talent.
